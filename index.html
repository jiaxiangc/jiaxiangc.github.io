I am a Researcher at Tencent HY, working with Heng Wang in the Video World Foundation Model Team. Previously, I worked with Qinglin Lu in the Video Application Model Team, and was a Research Intern at ByteDance Intelligent Creation Team, collaborating with Jie Wu. I received my Master's degree in Artificial Intelligence from Beihang University in 2025.
